    Devil Pirates

    Formed from the Remnants of the Krieg Pirates, the Devil Pirates are a crew from the East Blue who failed their initial voyage into the Grand Line after being destroyed by Mihawk. After a failed attack on the Baratie, their former captain, Don Krieg, tried to sacrifice them. After Krieg was defeated and arrested, the surviving members of the crew were inspired by Luffy and Samuel of the Straw Hat Pirates to continue being pirates, and conquer the Grand Line that defeated them before.

    Due to their respect of Luffy, they became a Subordinate Crew under the Straw Hat Grand Fleet. The Captain, Gin, holds the position of First Chair.

    Jolly Roger

    The Jolly Roger of the Devil Pirates is a Black Sea King over Black Bones on a White flag. The design was taken from Gin's signature coat. It was created by Usopp, a member of their allies, the Straw Hat Pirates, after the battle of Arlong Park.

    Crew Members

    Main Crew

    The crew is led by Gin, Krieg's former first mate who tried to defend the crew. He was voted into power after the crew was inspired by the Straw Hat Pirates to regroup and reform. Their first mate is Pearl, the former second mate of the Krieg Pirates. Their Quartermaster is Ideaman, who maintained is position after the transfer of power.

    Crew Strength

    Fighting Abilities

    As the Remnants of the second strongets crew in the East Blue, the Devil Pirates are stonger the average pirate, at least when compared to other crews in the East Blue. Their Officers have the following levels:

    • Gin: Warrior (Cursed Soul) 6
    • Pearl: Warrior 5
    • Ideaman: Marksman 5

    In addition to their officers, there are 20 members of the crew.

    Three members of the crew possess Haki, Gin, Pearl and Ideaman. Gin and Pearl have unlocked Armament Haki, while Gin and Ideaman have unlocked Observation Haki.

    The Crew has no Devil Fruit Users

    Alliances

    The Devil Pirates are a Subordinate Crew of the Straw Hat Pirates. Their leader, Gin, is the First Chair of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet.

    Weaknesses

    The First Mate, Pearl, is afraid of Fire.

    Most members of the crew do not possess Haki, making them unable to attack Logias.

    As of the Arlong Park Arc, this is not longer true, with Pearl unlocking Armament Haki and Gin unlocking both Armament and Observation.
