Straw Hat Pirates

The Straw Hat Pirates were formed by Monkey D. Luffy on his seventeenth Birthday. They are a rookie pirate crew from the East Blue. The crew draw their name from their captain's signature straw hat, which was given to him by "Red-Haired" Shanks.

The Straw Hat Pirates are also the leaders of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet, an alliance of Crew in support of making Luffy the Pirate King.

Jolly Roger

The Jolly Roger of the Straw Hat Pirates is a White Skull wearing a Straw Hat over White Bones on a Black flag. The design was taken from Luffy's signature hat. It was created by Usopp, a member of the crew, after he joined.

An earlier version of the Jolly Roger was created by Luffy, however it was so poorly drawn that Nami forbid Luffy from hanging it up.

Fighting Abilities

Despite having only ten members, the Straw Hat Pirates are one of the stongest in the East Blue. This is primarily due to their widespead usage of Haki. The levels of the crew, and their Haki abilities are below:

Captain & Commanders

  • Luffy: Bruiser (Black Fist) 4, Armament/Conquerors
  • Zoro: Warrior (Cursed Soul) 4, Armament/Observation
  • Samuel: Rogue (Swashbuckler) 3, Observation/Conquerors
  • Sanji: Hybrid (Germa) 3, Observation

Senior Officers

  • Nami: Tinkerer (Meteoroligist) 2, Observation
  • Charles: Warrior (Black Blade) 3, Armament/Conquerors
  • Usopp: Marksman 2 (Sniper), Observation

Members

  • Kaya: Chemist 1 (Geneticist), Observation
  • Johnny: Warrior 2 (Elemental Warrioe)
  • Yosaku: Warrior 2 (Black Blade)

Devil Fruit

There are two devil fruit users on the Crew, the Captain Luffy and the Quartermaster Samuel.
