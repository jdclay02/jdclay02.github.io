    Straw Hat Pirates

    The Straw Hat Pirates were formed by Monkey D. Luffy on his seventeenth Birthday. They are a rookie pirate crew from the East Blue. The crew draw their name from their captain's signature straw hat, which was given to him by "Red-Haired" Shanks.

    The Straw Hat Pirates are also the leaders of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet, an alliance of Pirate Crews in support of making Luffy the Pirate King. As of the Alabasta Arc, they are also allies of the Whitebeard Pirates.

    Jolly Roger

    The Jolly Roger of the Straw Hat Pirates is a White Skull wearing a Straw Hat over White Bones on a Black flag. The design was taken from Luffy's signature hat. It was created by Usopp, a member of the crew, after he joined.

    An earlier version of the Jolly Roger was created by Luffy, however it was so poorly drawn that Nami forbid Luffy from hanging it up.

    The Flag is also flow by the Subordinate Crews of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet.

    The Straw Hat Pirates have two subordinate crews, the Devil Pirates led by Gin and the Barto Club led by Bartolomeo.
