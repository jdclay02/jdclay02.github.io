    Straw Hat Pirates

    The Straw Hat Pirates were formed by Monkey D. Luffy on his seventeenth Birthday. They are a rookie pirate crew from the East Blue. The crew draw their name from their captain's signature straw hat, which was given to him by "Red-Haired" Shanks.

    The Straw Hat Pirates are also the leaders of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet, an alliance of Pirate Crews in support of making Luffy the Pirate King. As of the Alabasta Arc, they are also allies of the Whitebeard Pirates.

    Jolly Roger

    The Jolly Roger of the Straw Hat Pirates is a White Skull wearing a Straw Hat over White Bones on a Black flag. The design was taken from Luffy's signature hat. It was created by Usopp, a member of the crew, after he joined.

    An earlier version of the Jolly Roger was created by Luffy, however it was so poorly drawn that Nami forbid Luffy from hanging it up.

    The Flag is also flow by the Subordinate Crews of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet.

    Sub-Groups

    Though it is never explicitly stated, the chain of Command is Luffy -> Zoro -> Nami -> Samuel, with Samuel and Nami switching places depending if the crew is on Sea or Land respectivly.

    Terrirories

    The crew technically holds Alabasta as a territory as of the Post-Alabasta Arc. At the end of the Sky Island Saga, they gain Angel Island and Upper Yard as their territory. As of the Thriller Bark Saga, they gained the Florian Triangle and as of the Shiki Saga, they gained Merville.

    Alliances

    The Straw Hat Pirates have seven subordinate crews, the Devil Pirates led by Gin, the Barto Club led by Bartolomeo, the Giant Warrior Pirates led by Brogy and Dorry, the Saruyama Alliance led by Cricket, the Foxy Pirates led by Foxy, the Phoenix Pirates led by Puzzle and the Rolling Pirates led by Lola.

    They are also allies of the Whitebeard Pirates.

    Weaknesses

    Due to having six devil fruit users on the crew, the other members need to make sure they don'tdrown.
