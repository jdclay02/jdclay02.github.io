    Barto Club

    The Barto Club are a Pirate Crew formed from it's Captain, Bartolomeo's former Mafia family. They are a rookie pirate crew from the East Blue, with most of it's members hailing from Loguetown. They were inspired to head out to Sea after seeing Luffy survive his execution after a bolt of lightning saved him.

    As of the Warship Island Arc, they joined the Straw Hat Grand Fleet with their captain, Bartolomeo becoming the Second Chair of the Grand Fleet.

    Jolly Roger

    The Jolly Roger of the Barto Club is a screaming red skull with green hair, with a red X below it, on a black flag. The skull is to represent the captain, Bartolomeo who has green hair.

    Crew Members

    Main Crew

    The Crew currently consists of three officer, all from the East Blue, and former members of Bartolomeo's Mafia Family. The Crew is Led by Bartolomeo, with his childhood friend Desire serving as his first mate and his former underboss Gambia serving as his Quartermaster.
