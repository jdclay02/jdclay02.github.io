Goat Island

The Goat Island Arc is the first arc of the Sky Island Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Marine Chase

The Straw Hat's are being traces by three marine ships led by Minchey. When cannonballs are fired, Luffy bounces them back, destroying two of the ships before the crew escape into a fog bank.

Arrival

The crew arrive on Goat island, and meet Zenny after chasing some goats. Samuel informs the crew about his reputation as a pirate money-lender. They crew sticks around to help the old man out, until Minchey arrives. The Straw Hats defeat Minchey, and allow for his subordinates to leave and report him.

Captain Moore arrives a few days later, of thanks the Straw Hats. As a rewards, they will be given a headstart before he tries to capture them. The crew escape.

Story Impact

  • The crew meet and befriend Zenny.
