Dead End Race

The Dead End Race Arc is the second arc of the Sky Island Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Marine Chase

The Marines are chasing after the Going Merry, but they Straw Hats are able to escape from Lieutenant Commander Drake.

Dead End Race

What eating at a restaurant, they notice a pirate using two coins to enter a secret room, Robin is familiar with the custom, and informs the crew about the Dead End Race. Luffy decides he want's to join, so Robin leads the crew deeper into the island.

At the Pirate Cove, Nami signs up the crew for the race, while the rest of the crew explore. Luffy ends up in a fight after he steals food from Shuraiya Basc√πd. After the fight, Luffy rejects an alliance with Gasparde, while Shuraiya accepts joining him.

The Next Day, the race begins, and the crew sail off the starting waterfall while fighting off pirates. After leaving the starting island, the crew meets Anaguma and agree to look after her for the duration of the race.

After a few more encounters, they arrive at a Marine Stronghold, and realize that they have been tricked. A pissed off Nami guides the crew to Gasparde.

General Gaspard

The crew encounter Gasparde, and defeat him and his crew, allying with Shuraiya who was after him for destroying his home.

Story Impact
