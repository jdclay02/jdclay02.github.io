Skypiea

The Skypiea Arc is the sixth arc of the Sky Island Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Arrival in Skypiea

The Crew arrive on the White Sea, and are almost attacked by a Masked Man, who recognizes Cricket. Cricket and the Masked Man introduce themselves, and the masked man is revealed to by Wyper, a decendent of Calgara. Story of Noland and Calgara. Gan Fall arrives, and witnesses the alliance of Wyper of the Straw Hats.

With Wyper's help, they are able to enter the White White Sea without encountering Amazon. The crew go to stock up with Cricket and Wyper talk on Angel Island. The crew meet Conis and Pagaya, who explain Dials and the Waver. When Wyper comments on God not noticing them, Samuel reveals his Haki ability, Observation Killer, which prevents him and those around him from being targeted by Observation Haki aka Mantra.

The crew are acosted by the White Barrets, and defeat them. Conis reveals her deception, and is forgiven by the crew. When Gan Fall arrives to take Conis and Pagaya to safety, Conis refuses, as she wishes to help the Straw Hats to make up for betraying them.

The Ordeals

The crew arrive on Upper Yard. The group splits unto four squads, each following either Luffy or a commander into an ordeal. Luffy undergoes the Ordeal of Balls, Zoro the Ordeal of Iron, Samuel the Ordeal of Swamp and Sanji the ORdeal of String.

The Crew Reunite at the Merry, and it is revealed that Wyper left to contact the Shadorans. That night, the crew decide to party. During the party, Robin and Samuel discuss the possibility of a Poneglyph near upper yard. Luffy, Usopp, Kaya and Charles encounter the Klabautermann of Merry.

The Survival Game

The Next day, Enel declares the Survival Game to be in effect. The Straw Hats and Shandorans team up against Enel and his divine soldiers, with Robin taking out Yama. While Samuel and Robin are exploring the ruins, Usopp and Sanji face Enel aboard the Merry along with Johnny and Yosaku. Sanji awakens Conqueror's Haki.

In the ruins, the divine order have been defeated, and the remaining Straw Hats and Shandians prepare to fight Enel. It is revealed the Luffy is immune to electricity, and Luffy takes point in fighting Enel. During the fight, Luffy discovers the Golden Bell, and knocks Enel into it to defeat him.

Aftermath

The Skypians, Shandorans and Straw Hats have a massive party. Samuel ambushes Enel aboard the Ark and assassinates him, collecting his devil fruit. The next day, Robin, Luffy and Samuel read about the location of Poseidon.

On the fourth day, the Straw Hat's depart with Billions worth of Gold in Tow. The crew fall off the White Sea.

Story Impact

  • The Crew Defeat Enel and his Priests
  • Sanji awakens Conqueror's Haki
