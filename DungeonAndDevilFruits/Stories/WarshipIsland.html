Warship Island

The Warship Island Arc is the tenth arc of the East Blue Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Dragon Tamer

The crew find a young girl drifting on a piece of wood, and bring her aboard the ship so she can eat and rest. After befriending her, they learn of her capture and escape from the Marines of Branch 8. The crew agree to help her return to Warship island, and nearly enter the Calm Belt, before Nami notices and changes their heading.

Warship Island

The crew arrive on Warship Island, and learn the history of the island from one of the elders. While there, they also learn about Apis's devil fruit, and meet Ryu, her friend who is a Sennenryu. The crew agree's to help find Ryu's homeland. Sanji tells the crew that he recalls reading about the Lost Island and the Dragon Nest. A Flashback reveals that he and Samuel talked the previous night, and Samuel informed Sanji of it.

Dragon Nest

While waiting for Lost Island to Surface, the crew are attacked by Eric, a mercenary working for Marine Branch 8, as well as Captian Nelson Royale and Lieutenant Commander Hardy. The crew are nearly overwhelmed when a nearly-clear, green-tinted barrier blocks a barrage of cannonballs. Bartolomeo has arrived, and come to assist the Straw Hat's with his crew.

With the relief provided by the Barto Club, the Straw Hats regroup, and begin their counter assault, providing relief for the Devil Pirates before defeating the Marines.

The Straw Hats, Devils, and Clubers watch the Millenium Dragon migration in Awe. Luffy and Bartolomeo meet. Bartolomeo thanks Luffy for inspiring him. The three crews party. During the party, Gin and Samuel approach Bartolomeo about him becoming a member of the Grand Fleet, which he accepts.

Story Impact

  • The Straw Hat's get a Spirit Surge
  • Bartolomeo and the Barto Club join the Straw Hat Grand Fleet as the second chair.
