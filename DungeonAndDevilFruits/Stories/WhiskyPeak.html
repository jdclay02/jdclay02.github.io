Whisky Peak

The Whisky Peak Arc is the second arc of the Baroque Works Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Mysterious Town

The Straw Hat's get their first taste of Grand Line weather. Samuel reassures Nami that it will get better, as the current weather is due to the overlapping magnetic fields of the multiple starting islands at the beginning of the Grand Line.

The crew arrive at Whisky Peak, and Miss Wednesday tries to jump ship with her partner, only for the duo to be captured by Samuel's vines, and dragged below deck. The crew pull into port, and while initially suprised by the warm reception, quickly get into the moon and start to party. By nightfall, the crew has passed out except for Zoro, Nami, Samuel and Nojiko, who are faking it.

With the Bounty Hunter's thinking they are distracted, they try and collect the bounties, only to be stopped by Zoro. Zoro fights the millions while Samuel fights Mr. 8 and Mrs. Monday. Meanwhile, Nami and Nojiko steal from the town.

Baroque Works

As the fighting ends, two more Baroque works agents arrive, and state their mission to kill Mr. 8 and Miss Wednesday, who spies in Baroque Works. The fighting has awoken Luffy, who attacks Zoro for beating up the people who fed them. Samuel takes advantage of the fight between Luffy and Zoro to take out Mr. 5 and Miss Valentine.

Mr. 8, now named Igaram, asks Nami to protect and escort Vivi, which she agree's to for a fee of $1,000,000,000. When Vivi explains what Baroque works are, Nami admits to already knowing. When Mr. 13 and Miss Friday try and leave, Vivi notices them and panics. Samuel calls of for Usopp to shoot the vulture (Miss Friday), causing the sniper to wake up and shoot her out of the sky.

As the crew wake and return to the ship, they meet miss all sunday, who Samuel recognizes as Nico Robin.

Story Impact

  • The Crew, -vivi get a Rare Spirit Surge
  • Vivi joins the Straw Hat Pirates.
  • The crew meet Nico Robin.
