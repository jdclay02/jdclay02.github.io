Impel Down

The Impel Down Arc is the second arc of the Summit War Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Entering Hell

It is revealed Samuel was sent to Impel Down at his own request. While he is being processed, he destroys the seastone cuffs and creates fake ones using his Logia powers. He is brought down to level six. He walks past Garp, who is leaving after speaking with Ace. After he is chained to the wall, and the jailers leave, he replaces the chains with vines.

Samuel starts sending messages throughout the prison.

A Captain's Arrival

Luffy arrives in the Prison with Hancock, and allies with old enemies as he goes down the floors. This includes Buggy on floor 1, Galdino on floor 2, Bentham on floor 3, Daz Bonez on Floor 4, where he fights and is poisoned by Magellan.

Luffy is in level 5, and is brought to level 5.5 by Bentham, where Luffy is healed. Luffy recruits the Newkama, and heads to the Eternal Hell, where Samuel is. Samuel has formed alliances with Jinbe, Crocodile and Bullet.

The Breakout

The first mass breakout if Impel Down Begins. Floor 4, Blackbeard arrives. Floor 3, Magellan is defeated. Escape from Impel down on Marine ships, Reveal of Buggy and Bullet's past as Roger Pirates.
