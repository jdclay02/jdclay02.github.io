Cursed Sword

The Cursed Sword Arc is the fourth arc of the Sky Island Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Asuka Island

The Straw Hats arrive at Asuka Island. The crew split up, with one group staying on the ship while the others head into town for supplies. Robin recognizes the island, and remarks on the Red Moon in their legends. Back on the Merry, Zoro receives a dagger, and leaves the ship to find where it came from. Unknown to Zoro, Charles follows behind.

While the Strat Hats escape from Commander Drake, they search for Zoro and Charles. The crew manages to escape from the Marines, and at the same time Zoro and Charles arrive at a secret Marine Dojo.

The Straw Hats meet Maya, and follow her to her town. While the crew are there, marines attack the town, and the crew defeats the Marines. The crew follow Zoro back to the Dojo.

Zoro vs Saga

Zoro defeats Saga.
