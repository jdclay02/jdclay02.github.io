Gold Island

The Gold Island Arc is the Fourth arc in the East Blue Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits. Summary Summary Summary.

Summary

Band of Thieves

The Straw Hat's have lunch, cooked by Charles. While not particularly good, it is edible. Luffy designs a flag for the ship, which doesn't look that good, so Usopp takes a crack at it, first designing aa flag for himself before designed a good looking Flag for the Straw Hat Pirates. As the crew is cleaning up, they are attacked by the Hyena brothers, who are pushed off the ship. Eldoraggo then appears, and capsizes the Merry with a scream.

Usopp, Kaya and Nami are swept away, while Chales rescues Samuel, and Zoro rescues Luffy and Tobio. Luffy's group come upon a floating Oden cart run by Tobio's Grandfather, which they eat at. From Ganzo, they learn about Woonan and his treasure. When Ganzo tries to get the Straw Hats to pay, they flee.

The Isle of Gold

Nami's group are captured along with the Merry by Eldoraggo after landing on the Isle of Gold. Usopp offers to decipher the map to protect the crew, which Eldoraggo accepts. They eventually reach a castle where the treasure is supposedly buried. When Eldoraggo starts using his power to destroy the surrounding area. The noise from this alerts Luffy's group, which allows them to find the rest of the crew.

Luffy and Zoro distract Eldoraggo and Golass while Samuel and Charles rescue the crew and retreat. Luffy and Zoro are also forced to retreat, primarily to lead their opponents away from the crew. With the crew rescued, they reunite on a hill and check over the stolen map.

Following an initially misinterpereted clue, leads them to a Plateau where they meet back up with Tobio. Scaling the cliff, they locate Woonan's cave, and find Ganzo there. From Ganzo, they learn the story of Woonan. Climbing up the rest of the way, they find an isolated Hut, and are attacked by Eldoraggo.

The Screaming Pirates

Luffy faces off against Eldoraggo, while Zoro faces Golass, and the rest of the Straw Hats take on the other members of the Screaming Pirates.

With the Screaming Pirates defeated, they meet back up with Ganzo and Tobio, and discover the Treasure of Woonan, his deathbed letter to Ganzo. With the mystery solved, the Straw Hat's depart the island, but not before looting Eldoraggo's ship.
