Amazon Lily

The Amazon Lily Arc is the second arc of the summit War Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Arrival on the Island

Luffy arrives in Amazon Lily, and eats a bad mushroom. He is brough to the Kuja village and latter identified as a man by elder Nyon. Luffy in improsoned. Luffy steals back his hat, and gets clothes. Luffy escapes with Margurite's help.

Snake Princess

Luffy accedently ends up in Hancock's bath. Luffy is immune to Boa's powers. Luffy tries to escape but fails. Luffy is brought to the Arena. Luffy scares Bacura and defeats Sandersonia and Marigold. Luffy asks for the girls who rescued him to be reverted.

Back at the Castle, Luffy eats and learns of the Slave Brand. Luffy learns Hancock's story. Boa falls for Luffy, and agrees to help him get into Impel Down.

Story Impact

  • Luffy forms an alliance with Boa
