Rainbow Mist

The Rainbow Mist Arc is the third arc of the Sky Island Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Ruluka Island

The Straw Hat's arrive in Ruluka island, and become enemies of the Mayor. The crew meet professor Henzo, and learn about the Rainbow Mist.

Rainbow Mist

The Crew enter the Rainbow Mist, and meet the Pumpkin Pirates, the childhood friends of Henzo. With the assistance from the Pumpkin Pirates, the crew manages to escape from the Rainbow Mist.

Back in the real world, the Mayor uses the rainbow tower to make a bridge to the Rainbow Mist. The crew take out the Mayor with his goons as the Rainbow Mist collapses. As the crew escapes the island, the Mayor is arrested by the Marines.

Story Impact

  • The crew temporarily ally with the Pumpkin Pirates led by Rapanui Pasqua
