Post-Alabasta

The Post-Alabasta Arc is the seventh and final arc of the Baroque Works Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Aftermath

The Straw Hats rest, recover and party. Smoker, Tashigi and their subordinates join for the first night, but leave after that. Samuel lives Ace an abriged copy of the instructive guide to Haki.

The newspaper arrives, and the crew see their new bounties. Zoro and Sanji get their first bounties, as well as Vivi.

  • "Strawhat" Monkey D. Luffy - $225,000,000
  • "Eldritch Beast" Avery D. Samuel = $125,000,000
  • "Demon" Roronoa Zoro = $111,000,000
  • "Black Leg" Sanji = $93,000,000
  • "Coursair Princess" Nefertari Vivi = $100,000,000
  • "Devil Man" Gin = $127,000,000
  • "Iron Wall" Pearl = $66,000,000
  • "Black" Bartolomeo = $43,000,000
  • "Sweet" Desire = $22,000,000

Farewell

The Straw Hat Pirates leave Alabasta with Vivi. Cobra meets with Sabo, and joins the Revolutionary Army.

The New Crewmember

After leaving Alabasta, Robin reveals herself, and invites herself onto the crew. Samuel gives her a Den Den Mushi and a number. Robin and Saul speak.

Story Impact

  • The Crew gets New Bounties
  • Robin joins the crew
