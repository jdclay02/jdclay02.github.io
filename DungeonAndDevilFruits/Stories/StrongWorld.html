Strong World

The Strong World Arc is the Second arc of the Shiki Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Merville

The crew assist Shiki with warning of a Storm, before Nami is Kidnapped. The crew are then brought to Merville, and scattered amongst the Islands. The crew defends themselves and consumes the monsterous creatures. Most of the crew reunite, at a secret village protected by Daft Green.

Nami manages to escape from Shiki with Billy's help, and encounters Luffy. The crew learn about Shiki's plan for the East Blue. When Nami and Luffy arrive at the Village, and are informed of Shiki's plan to attack the East Blue.

Shiki arrives, and takes back Nami, while fending off the Straw Hats. Nami leaves a Tone Dial for the crew, which ends with Nami asking for the crew to Rescue her.

Sakazuki

During the Sakazuki Ceremony, it is revealed that Gin, Bartolomeo, Cricket and Puzzle are in Attendence, and interrupt it by telling Shiki to fuck off. The Straw Hats come in, and start shooting up the place.

Usopp vs Indigo, Chopper heals Nami, Robin vs Scarlet. Charles, Franky and Brook vs Golden Lion Grand Fleet. Luffy, Zoro, Samuel and Sanji vs Shiki. Luffy gains Legendary Conquerors, Zoro gains Legendary Armament, Samuel awakens his fruit, Sanji gains Legendary Observation.

Aftermath

The Marines capture the Golden Lion Grand Fleet. Puzzle moves Merville to the Florian Archipelago.
