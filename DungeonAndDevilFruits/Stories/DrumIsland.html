Drum Island

The Drum Island Arc is the fourth arc of the Baroque Works Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Wapol the Evil King

The Straw Hat's make a detour to Drum Island due to Vivi's condition. While she is unconcious, Samuel apologises to her, for not preventing her illness. On the way there, they are attacked by Wapol, who is defeated along with his soldiers.

Pulling into Drum Kingdom, Dalton tries to turn them away, but Luffy is able to convince him to allow them to dock to take Vivi to a doctor. Sanji and Luffy take Vivi up the Drum Rockies to the castle.

The Reindeer Doctor

Luffy and Sanji fight off Lapahns to get to the castle, where they meet Kureha and Chopper. While Luffy and Sanji befriend Chopper, the rest of the crew hold of Wapol's crew when he returns.

At the Castle, Luffy and Sanji learn Chopper's backstory. Soon after, Wapol, his brother, and his men arrive at attack. Luffy, Sanji and Chopper defeat Wapol and his men.

Cherry Blossoms

Luffy recruits Chopper, and Sanji gets out of debt by stating Chopper is the Doctor in charge of Vivi, and as he is coming with them, they will pay Chopper.
