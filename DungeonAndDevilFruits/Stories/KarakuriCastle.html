Karakuri Castle

The Karakuri Castle Arc is the seventh arc of the Water 7 Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Mecha Island

Crew rescue Roba in a storm, and get a Turtle Pose. Crew arrive at island, and learn of the Golden Crown, as well as Mecha Island. Ratchet and General Maji attack the crew. The crew fight back and defeat Rachet and his forces. Roba asks the crew to find the Treasue.

Treasure Hunt

Crew search for treasure and defeat Rachet's crew. Free turtle and take hatches egg shell.

Story Impact

  • Rare Spirit surge.
