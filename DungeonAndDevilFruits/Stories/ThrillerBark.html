Thriller Bark

The Thriller Bark Arc is the second arc of the Thriller Bark Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Devil's Sea

The Straw Hats arrive in the Florian Triagle, and meet Brook. Luffy invites Brook the the crew. Singalong for Binks Sake. Brook explains his situation, with his shadow being stolen. The Thousand Sunny is captured by Thriller Bark, and Brook leaves.

The Crew explore Thriller Bark in several groups. Luffy's group are asked to defeat Moria and reclaim the Shadows of the Zombies. The Crew end up Fighting the General Zombies. Moria awakens and manages to claim Luffy's Shadow.

Coutdown to Sunrise

Oars awakens with Lufffy's shadow. The crew learn Laboon was Brook's friend. Sanji vs Absalom, Zoro vs Ryuuma, Chopper vs Hogback, Usopp vs Perona.

Crew vs Oars. Kuma arrives. Nightmare Luffy vs Oars. Luffy vs Shadow Asgard. Zoro vs Kuma. Zoro unlocks Conquerors Haki when nothing happens.

Aftemath

Brook officially joins the crew. Alliance with Lola. Celebration Party. Funeral of Ryuuma and the Rumbar Pirates. Zoro, Samuel, Sanji and Nami share Sake Cups. Learn of Aces capture by Blackbeard.

Story Impact

  • Very Rare Spirit Surge
  • Brook Joins the Straw Hats
  • Lola becomes a member of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet, with Thriller Bark and the Florian triangle as her territory.

Trivia

  • Luffy reaches level 12
  • Zoro, Samuel and Sanji reach level 11
  • Nami, Charles, Usopp, Chopper, Robin and Franky reach level 9
  • Kaya, Johnny, Yosaku, Nojiko, Vivi and Conis reach level 8
