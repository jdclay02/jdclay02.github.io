Baratie

The Baratie Arc is the fifth arc in the East Blue Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Bounty Hunters

Usopp and Luffy test out the new Cannons and Cannonballs gained from the Screaming pirates. While testing the Cannon, they break apart a rock and find Johnny and Yosaku, two bounty hunters who used to work with Zoro. Taking the bounty hunters aboard, Kaya diagnoses Yosaku with Scurvy, and works to cure him. Once the two are feeling better, they mention the Baratie, the Sea Restaurant.

The Floating Resturaut

As the crew arrive at the Baratie, they are fired upon by a Marine ship, which Luffy bounces back, sinking the ship. The crew enter the resturaut, and are waited upon by Sanji, until Marine Lieutenant Fullbody arrives and tries to pick a fight with Luffy. Luffy scares away the Marine with Conquerer's Haki, making Zeff take notice of the young crew.

While the crew are eating, Gin arrives and is fed by Sanji. Luffy decides he want's to recruit Sanji to the crew. Enjoying the food, the crew agree to stay and wait.

The Black Blade

Two days later, the crew find Nami and the Merry missing. There is a flashback to Samuel confronting Nami before dawn, where they play the Drinking Question Game, Nami will stay if Samuel wins, but Samuel won't stand in her way if she wins. Nami wins with her last question being that Samuel already knew about her situation with Arlong. Samuel drinks, and tells her that he's known for over ten years, confusing her. Samuel promises Nami that should she need help, the crew will be there for her.

Don Krieg arrives and demands food. Zeff provides it, and Krieg tries to take over the restaurant. Mihawk arrives and destroys Krieg's ship. Zoro challenges Mihawk to a duel, which is accepted. Mihawk initially unsheathes Kogatama, but switches to Yoru after sensing that Zoro possesses Haki. Mihawk wins the duel, but allows Zoro to live after impressing him, and tells Zoro to come find him when he is stronger.

After Mihawk leaves, Usopp, Kaya, Zoro, Charles and Johnny set out to follow after Nami. Luffy, Samuel and Yosaku stay behind to fight the Krieg Pirates. Samuel asks Luffy if he can fight the crew, but Luffy claims Krieg for himself. Luffy defeats Krieg and six of his crew while Samuel takes out Pearl and 20 of his crew by shifting into his elemental form. Meanwhile, Sanji defeats Gin.

With the Krieg Pirates defeated, the leave Krieg with Fullbody, and Samuel convinces Gin to ally with the Straw Hats as leader of his own crew. Gin accepts hesitantly, until Luffy encourages him. Later, Sanji joins the crew after heart to heart with Zeff.

The Four Straw Hats (Luffy, Samuel, Yosaku and Sanji) set of for Cocoyashi with Gin and the newly formed Devil Pirates in tow.
