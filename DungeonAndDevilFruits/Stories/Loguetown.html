Loguetown

The Loguetown Arc is the eigth arc in the East Blue Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Decks of the World

Makino and Woopslap look over Luffy's bounty, Woop Slap is annoyed by it, while Makino is happy for Luffy. Mihawk meets with Shanks, and shows him Luffy's bounty.

The Town of the Beginning

The crews arrive in Loguetown. Samuel gives the Devil Pirates some money for they can go an buy a ship. Samuel and Sanji leave to go an procure supplies while the rest of the crew split up to go off on their own. While they are buying food, Sanji notices Samuel signaling one of the merchants.

Zoro and Charles head to a weapon shop to get new swords, when they come across and help Tashigi. Zoro recieves the Sandai and Yubashiri, while Charles gets a normal sword. Meanwhile, Nami, Nojiko and Kaya shop for new clothes, looking for things that will be durable enough for the grand line. Usopp gets some new goggles and searches for a Den Den Mushi at the request of Samuel. Johnny and Yosaku guard the ship.

Samuel gets contacted, and takes Sanji to a bar. There, they meet Bello Betty and Monkey D. Dragon of the Revolutionary Army. Samuel enters negotiations with Dragon for two things, the first is for Chief of Staff Sabo to meet with the Straw Hat crew at Alabasta, and the second is for the Den Den Mushi number of Jaguar D. Saul. Dragon asks what's in it for him, and Samuel jokes that it's to help his son, which is true, before stating that it will get Sabo's memories back. Samuel also offers a few pieces of information in exchange.

Samuel's Offered information
  • 1: Imu Nerona is still alive and rules the World Government. The Gorosei answer to them.
  • 2: York, one of Vegapunk's bodies is designing superweapons for the World Government behind Vegapunk's back.
  • 3: Ivankov is on level 5.5 of Impel Down.

    • Dragon accepts the information, and asks Samuel if he is a time traveler or a dimension hopper. Samuel responds the latter, and leaves with Sanji. As they walk away, Sanji asks what Samuel's plan is for this world, and how much Charles knows. Samuel informs Sanji that while Charles is also from another dimension, he was unaware of the world before coming to it. As for Samuel's plans, he repeats a modified quote of Roger "Wealth, Fame, Power, I want to see everything this world has to offer."

    The Town of the Ending

    Luffy collides with Buggy on his way to the executioners platform, and gets directions without either man recognizing the other, at least not immidietly for Buggy. Luffy climbs to the Top of the Executioners stand, and is yelled at to get down by a marine when Buggy attacks him. Zoro, Sanji, Samuel, Charles, Usopp, Nami, Kaya and Nojiko are present when they see Luffy smile, before he is saved by a bolt of lightning. Samuel, who is standing next to Bartolomeo and Desire, cheers for his captain.

    Smoker and Tashigi arrive, with Luffy and Zoro going to fight them while Samuel and Sanji fight Alvida and Buggy. During the fight, Samuel brings up Buggy's past as a member of the Roger pirates, and questions him being this weak, as he's not using Haki. Buggy states that he left the grand line to get away from that craziness. Samuel offers Buggy an alliance, which he declines, leading to him and Alvida being knocked out. Soon after, Luffy and Zoro defeat their opponents, and meet Dragon.

    Dragon gives the crew a good wind, and the crew sets off for Reverse Mountain. Aboard the ship, they declare their dreams, with the Devil Pirates close behind them.

    Story Impact

    • Due to Samuel's actions, Bartolomeo and Desire set off together, a year earlier than before, at least in Bartolomeo's case.
    • After being betten by Luffy, Smoker sets out to follow him to the Grand Line.
    • Samuel meets Dragon, and gets his help arranging a meeting between Sabo, Ace and Luffy. Luffy also meets Dragon, after he beats Smoker.
    • Luffy gets a Spirit Surge (Rare), Zoro, Samuel and Sanji get a Spirit Surge (Uncommon)
