Syrup Village

The Syrup Village Arc is the third arc in the East Blue Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits. The Straw Hats arrive at Syrup village, and meet Usopp. Through Usopp they meet Kaya, and try to get a ship from her.

Summary

The Son of Yasopp

The Straw Hat Pirate arrive in Syrup Village, and meet Usopp, the town liar. Through Usopp, they meet the Usopp Pirates, and learn about Kaya. Going to visit Kaya, they meet her as well as her butlers Klahadore and Merry. When Klahadore tried to get Usopp and the crew to leave, he calls Usopp's father a pathetic pirate, which causes Samuel to burst out laughing, and inform everyone that Usopp's father is 'Chaser' Yasopp, one of the Commanders of the 'Red Hair' Yonko Fleet, with a bounty over 1 billion berry.

After leaving the Manor, the crew runs into Jango, who's power knocks out all but Zoro and Chanles, who fake falling asleep until Jango is out of sight. After waking up the rest of the crew, Luffy, Usopp and Charles went after Jango while Samuel, Nami and Zoro went into town to pick up some supplies.

The Black Cat Pirates

That Night, the crew meets up to discuss what they learned and agree to assist Usopp with protecting the village. Usopp plans to trap the beach, and Samuel suggests the other beach to be more likely, which Nami agree's with. They trap the beach and move Nami's ship. While the crew does this, Samuel teaches Usopp the basics of Haki.

The Black Cat Pirates arrive, and are defeated by the crew and Usopp. Jango is forced to summon Sham and Buchi, who are engaged by Zoro and Charles respectivly. Samuel goes up against Jango while Nami breaks into the Bezen Black and loots it.

When Khaladore, now revealed to be Kuro of a Thousand Plans, he finds that his entire crew has been defeated, and is brought into conflict with Luffy. Kaya arrives, and is protected by Usopp while Luffy defeats Kuro.

A Real Pirate

The crew follows Usopp and Kaya back to her Manor, where they check on a recovering Merry. While checking on Kaya, Samuel locates her 'Medicine' which he notices it actually Rat Poison. Samuel brings this up to Kaya, who is pissed off. Later, when Kaya gives the Merry to the Crew, and Luffy invites Usopp, Kaya requests to join as well, which Luffy accepts.

Story Impact

  • The Crew Experiance a Spirit Surge (-Kaya)
  • Usopp Joins the Crew at level 2, and unlocks Observation Haki
  • Kaya joins the crew at level 1
