Devil Tower

The Devil Tower Arc is the Second arc in the East Blue Saga of Dunegons and Devil Fruits. The Straw Hat's encounter a Plesiosaur and an enslaved village, and defeat the Ganzack Pirates to free them.

Summary

Attack of the Plesiosaur

The Straw Hat Pirates are traveling towards the Gecko Islands on their way to Loguetown, when they are attacked by a Plesiosaur. The five pirates manage to fight off the sea-dwelling dinosaur, however it manages to kidnap Nami as it escapes.

Not willing to let the beast take their crewmate, the four remaining Straw Hats give chase, and follow the beast to a nearby island. Beaching their ship, they are confronted by two Pirates that Samuel recognizes as part of Ganzack's crew, a pirate with a bounty of $10,000,000. The Four take out the two pirates and meet Medaka, a local girl.

At the village, the Straw Hats are introduced to Medaka's grandfatherm Skid. Skid tells of the Pirate Ganzack and his enslaving of the island. After a minor earthquake disturbs Luffy's meal, the crew head to the tower to rescue Nami.

The Devil's Tower

The crew break into the tower with Medaka, and locate an Armory. The Avery Brothers use the opporunity to gear up, picking up some new armor and weapons. Heading deeper into the tower, they locate Medaka's father, Herring. Medaka reveals the crew, leading to a fight. During the fight, Medaka is kidnapped by a masked figure.

Luffy leaves to chase after Medaka, while the rest of the crew fight against the Ganzack pirates, only being subdued after they threaten to fire on the slaves. Meanwhile, Luffy fights Ganzack and his Plesiosaur, and is an even match for them until he is captured in Foam.

Captured, the Straw Hats are forced to watch as the Ganzack pirates eat and party. When an explosion caused by Herring rocks the tower, Samuel escapes and he and Nami free the crew. Rushing down the tower, the crew interrupt the Execution of Herring and start beating down the Ganzack Pirates.

Ganzack calls for a retreat, and his Plesiosaur pulls the Devil Tower away from the island. The Straw Hat's give chase, with Luffy and Samuel pulling themselves as well as Zoro and Charles towards Ganzack's ship. Aboard, Luffy defeats Ganzack, Zoro defeat's Ganzack's first mate, Samuel defeats the Plesiosaur, and Samuel defeats the remaining crew.

Rewards and Requisitions

The Villagers gift the Straw Hats with some of Ganzack's treasure, as well as some food for them to continue their journey. Medaka gifts Luffy a life preserver. As they leave, Samuel reminds the civilians to contact the Marines.

Story Impact

  • The crew undergo a Spirit Surge.
  • Luffy unlocks Conqueror's Haki after having it explained to him the previous Arc by Samuel.
  • Zoro awakens Armament Haki due to training with Charles.
  • Nami awakens Obervation Haki due to training with Samuel

Trivia

  • Luffy, Zoro and Samuel reach level 3
  • Nami reaches level 2
