Long Ring Long Land

The Long Ring Long Land Arc is the first arc of the Water 7 Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Davy Back Fight

The Straw Hats arrive on Long Ring Long Land. The crew split up, and some of the Straw Hats meet Tonjit and Shelly. Meanwhile, the rest of the crew encounter Foxy. Foxy challenges Luffy to a Davy Back Fight, which he accepts.

Davy Back Fight

Straw Hat's win the first round, and see the Foxy Pirates cheating. The Second Round, they win again after cheating themselves. In the third round, they win again. After several more rounds, Luffy tkaes all of Foxy's crew members, before taking his flag and making him a member of the Grand Fleet.

The Admiral

Encounter with Aokiji, who defeats Luffy.

Story Impact

  • The Crew encounter an Admiral
  • Rare Spirit Surge
  • The Foxy Pirates join as the Fifth Seat of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet
