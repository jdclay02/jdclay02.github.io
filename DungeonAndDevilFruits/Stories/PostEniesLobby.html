Post-Enies Lobby

The Post-Enies Lobby Arc is the tenth and final arc of the Water 7 Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Revelations

Franky Family and Galley-La begin construction on the Thousand Sunny with the $1,000,000,000 they stole form the Straw Hats. Garp arrives to berate Luffy, and informs the crew of Luffy's father, which most were already aware of. Garp explains the four emperors. Reunion with Koby and Helmeppo. Samuel warns Garp about Blackbeard inheriting the will of Rocks D Xebec.

Straw Hat Party with Giants, Galley-La and Franky Family. Robin and Aokiji talk. Afterwards, Samuel speaks with Kuzan, and tells him that if he changes him mind about the Marines, he would be welcome amongst the Straw Hats.

The Crew receive new bounties, along with their Fleet and Franky.
