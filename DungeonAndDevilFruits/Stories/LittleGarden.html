Little Garden

The Little Garden Arc is the third arc of the Baroque Works Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Prehistoric Island

The crew arrive on Little Garden, and decide to go exploring. Samuel rests on the ship with Nami, Nojiko, Usopp and Kaya while Luffy goes to explore with Vivi, Johnny and Yosaku. Meanwhile, Zoro, Sanji and Charles go out hunting after getting bug spray from Samuel. Once the hunting party sets out, Samuel leaves as well to track down Baroque Works, killing a few dinosaurs along the way.

Duel of the Giants

Luffy's group runs into and befriends Dorry. At the same time, Broggy finds the ship and hangs out with Nami's group. Brogy inspires Usopp with tales of Elbaf, while Luffy notices that their beer has been messed with with his Observation Haki.

While the Giants go to have their duel, Samuel runs into Mr. 5 and Mrs. Valentine again, and defeats them again. Meanwhile, Zoro is attacked by Miss Goldenweek and defeats her while Luffy is attacked by Mr. 3, and defeats him.

While the rest of the crew deal with Baroque Works, Sanji locates thier base, and gets an eternal pose from a ship of billions, after he defeats them (Due to Mr. 13 being defeated earlier.)

After the Battle

Samuel meets the Giants, and calls them out for letting their crew be enslaved by the world government. Dorry and Brogy are upset, and don't believe Samuel until Samuel asks them to use their Observation Haki to sense any lies in his words. Now believing him, they agree to meet with the Straw Hats at Water 7, and become allies.

It is revealed that Smoker and Tashigi were listening in on call between Crocodile and Sanji, and have captured Mr. 11.

Story Impact

  • The Crew get a Rare Spirit Surge
  • The Giant Warrior Pirates become the Third Chair of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet
  • The Straw Hat's get an Eternal Pose to Alabasta
  • Vivi contracts Primal Kestia

Trivia

  • Luffy, Zoro and Samuel hit level 6
