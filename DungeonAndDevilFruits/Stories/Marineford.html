Marineford

The Marineford Arc is the fourth arc of the Summit War Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Calm before the Storm

Execution of Ace is to be broadcast to the world. Sengoku reveals the truth about Ace. Whitebeard arrives. Whitebeard vs Marines.

Arrival

Luffy, Samuel and the Impel Down Prisoners fall from the Sky. Luffy and Whitebeard meet face to face for the first time. Whitebeard notices Luffy's hat, and Luffy confirms it's origin. Samuel calls for the Straw Hat Grand Fleet to arrive.

Jinbe defeats Moria, Ivankov defeats Kuma, Buggy fights Mihawk. The Pacifista army arrive. Samuel gives Whitebeard a week of concentratied life essence. Samuel pulls Squardo back from Whitebeard, and redirects him. Whitebeard tilts Marineford. Luffy engages the Admirals.

Doflamingo vs Crocodile. Whitebeard vs Akainu. Samuel activates his awakening. Garp is KO'ed by Luffy. Luffy frees Ace.

The Final Order

Retreat. Akainu taunts Ace, but Ace ignores it thanks to Samuel's words in Impel Down. When Akainu tries to attack from the back, he is stopped by Dragon and Sabo. The Revolutionaries have arrives to put a stop to the Tyranny of executing a child for the crimes of their parents. Whitebeard defeats Akainu. Blackbeard and his crew arrive and pile on Whitebeard. Ace incinerates Whitebeards Corpse, after he dies using an awakened earthquake on Marineford.

Shanks arrives, and ends the war.
