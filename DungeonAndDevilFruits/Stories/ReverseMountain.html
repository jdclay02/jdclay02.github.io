Reverse Mountain

The Reverse Mountain Arc is the First arc of the Baroque Works Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Reverse Mountian

The crews arrive at the Red Line, and travel up it via Nami's navigation skills as Luffy's body bouncing the ships away from the sides of the channel. Cresting the top of the mountain the crews can see the world streaching out before them. Falling back down into the current, they head down the mountain and encounter a Giant Whale, and are eaten by it.

Crocus and the Whale

Inside the while, the Straw Hat's freak out over the missing Luffy, and locate Crocus. After a brief conversation, in which Samuel introduces Crocus as the Doctor of the Roger Pirates, they are show a way out. While leaveing, Laboon starts ramming his head into the Red Line, revealing Mr. 9 and Miss Wednesday of Baroque Works along with Luffy.

Zoro is the one to reveal his knowledge of Baroque Works, as he was offered a spot in the organization, and killed Mr. 7, scaring Mr. 9 and Miss Wednesday. Samuel elaborates on this, stating that Baroque Works is led by Sir Crocodile of the Warlords, and they have the goal of Conqueroring Alabasta.

Twin Capes

Crocus explains the hisotry of Laboon, and how his crew left the grand line. Samuel corrects him, as the Rumbar pirates were actually killed by the Rocks Pirates in the Florian Triangle leaving only the violinist, Brook, alive as he ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi, allowing him to resurrect himself. Luffy's response to this is to challenge Laboon, and paint the Straw Hat Jolly Roger on him.

Samuel gives the Baroque Works Log Pose to Bartolomeo, and explains to Desire how it works (Gin already has one from Loguetown). Samuel gets notes on Roger's illness to pass on to Ace. The three crews of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet split up. As they are leaving, they find Mr. 9 and Miss Wednesday aboard, and agree to take them to Whisky Peak.

Story Impact

  • The Straw Hats, Devil Pirates and Barto Club enter the Grand Line.
  • The Straw Hat Grand Fleet meet Laboon.
  • Crocus, the doctor of the Roger Pirate is introduced.
  • The Straw Hats agree to take the Baroque Works agent to Whisky Peak.
