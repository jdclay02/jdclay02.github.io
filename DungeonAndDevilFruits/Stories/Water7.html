Water 7

The Water 7 is the eighth arc of the Water 7 Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

City of Water

Crew meet Kokoro and her granddaughter, get show to Water 7. Citizens inform them of where to put their ship. Crew Split up. Luffy, Nami, Nojiko, Samuel, Usopp and Kaya go to turn in gold and get $1,000,000,000 in exchange. Luffy, Nami and Samuel go to Galley-La, while Usopp, Nojiko and Kaya return to the Sunny with the Gold.

Robin, Chopper, Sanji, Conis and Vivi wander around and explore. Zoro, Charles, Johnny and Yosaku guard the ship. Bounty Hunters are defeated after trying to Attack Crew. Usopp is beaten up by Franky Family. Meanwhile, Luffy's group visit Galley-La and get Kaku to check the Merry. Kaku later returns, and states the merry is unfixable.

The crew find Usopp, Kaya and Nojiko, causing the Commanders and Luffy to destroy the Franky Family. Once they return, Luffy decides that they need a new ship.

Leaving Friends Behind

Due to Luffy's decision to get a New Ship, Usopp disagrees, and fights Luffy that night, while Kaya cries on the ship. Robin's farewell to Chopper, Conis, Vivi and Sanji.

Masked Assassins

CP9 fail to assassinate Iceburg and burn down Galley-La. They then go against Franky. Meanwhile, the Straw Hats meet with Iceburg, and get absolved.

Franky's Past.

Sanji and Samuel break into the Sea Train, and fight agains Cipher Pol. Usopp, aka Sogeking joins Sanji and Samuel to help save Robin. The Straw Hats form an alliance with the Franky Family and Galley-La, and steal the Rocketman.
