Alabasta

The Alabasta Arc is the sixth arc of the Baroque Works Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Okama, The Pirate and The Revolutionary

The crew run into Bon Kurei on the way to Alabasta. He steals the faces of most of the crew, minus Sanji, Vivi and Samuel. Samuel recognizes his fruit as formerly belonging to Kurozumi Higurashi. Samuel calls him out for being a member of Baroque Works, and offers for him to defect. He considers it, but is forced to decline, as he gave his word.

The crew arrives in Nanohana, and Luffy rushes off after sensing Ace. Samuel follows after him, and watches the confrontation between Ace, Luffy and Smoker. Samuel watches the brothers play with Smoker, with Ace commenting on Luffy's use of Haki. After Smoker is defeated, the crew meet back on on the Going Merry, and are introduced to Ace. Ace offers Luffy to become a subordinate of Whitebeard, which is refused. Samuel speaks up, and suggests an Alliance, as Whitebeard has no intrest in the One Piece, which Ace refutes. Samuel counters that Whitebeard knows how to get to Laugh Tale, and just hasn't. Ace calls Whitebeard, who approves the Alliance, and his lack in intrest in the One Piece.

Just after the crew hangs up, Sabo asks for permission to come aboard. Ace and Luffy freak out, as he is alive. Sabo introduces his companions, Koala and Hack. Sabo also outs Samuel as being from another world with knowledge of the Future. Samuel defends himself, and Sanji sticks up for him, as Luffy would not want spoilers, to which the captain agrees. Vivi sends a message with Karoo, and convinces Ace and Sabo to help free her country.

The Desert

The Crew and their Allies anchor their ships on the west side of the Sandora River. They fight off the Kung-Fu Dugongs, which Samuel debates trying to recruit before decideing against it. The group passes through Erumalu, and defeat a Great Sandora Lizard after passing Warusagi Birds. The Ally with a Camel, and arrive at Yuba.

That night, Luffy, Sabo and Ace dig up a well for Toto. The Next day, Sabo offers to go and meet with Kohza on Vivi's behalf. Vivi gratefully accepts. Ace decides to stick with Sabo so they can catch up more.

Rainbase

The group arrive at Rainbase, and encounter Smoker with Tashigi. The crew split up, are mostly captures (Luffy, Smoker, Zoro, Vivi, Usopp, Nami). Samuel forms a temporary alliance with Tashigi to rescue the crew and Smoker from Crocodile, who has betrayed the World Government.

With Sanji causing a distraction, the crew are able to enter an beat up the Bananawani. Zoro cuts open the Seastone Cage, and the group escape. The Straw Hats form a temporary alliance with Smoker, Tashigi and the Marines under his command. Samuel informs Smoker of the location of Crocodiles backup bomb.

Ace, Sabo, Koala and Hack meet with Kohza and the other Rebel leaders, and inform them about the truth of Crocodile's plan. May of the Rebel leaders are unsure, but Kohza is relieved to hear that Cobra is innocent.

On the way to Alubarna, Luffy fights Crocodile, and the two seem to be evenly matches, as Luffy's Haki allows him to strike Crocodile, however he was caught offguard by Crocodiles ability to drain moisture.

The Desert War

The Officer agents try and catch Vivi, but are decieved. Zoro vs Mr. 1, Sanji vs Mr. 2, Samuel vs Mr. 3, Nami vs Ms. Doublefinger, Chopper and Nojiko vs Miss Goldenweek, Usopp and Kaya vs Mr. 4 and Miss Merry Christmas, Charles vs Mr. 5 and Miss Valentines, Johnny and Yosaku vs Millions.

Luffy vs Crocodile. Robin reads the Alabasta Ponyglyph.

Story Impact

  • The Crew get a Very Rare Spirit Surge.
  • The Straw Hats ally with the Emperor Whitebeard.
