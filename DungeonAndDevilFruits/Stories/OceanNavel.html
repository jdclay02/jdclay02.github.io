Ocean Navel

The Ocean Navel is the seventh arc in the East Blue Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Ocean's Navel

Samuel is sparing with Sanji when Charles locates a chest chained to a rock nearby. Nami takes the crew on a detour there, and after opening the chests, finds a skull that acts as a map to the Ocean's Navel, the location of a treasure which can make someone's dream come true.

Sanji leads Nami, Usopp, Charles and Kaya down in a diving bell, with Luffy and Samuel in charge of keeping the bell's rope crank constant. Down with the bell, the crew lands and discovers large monsters. Sanji tries to fight one, but is unable to defeat it. The group is found by a talking bat that offers to lead the to the treasure.

Navel of the Sea

The Going Merry falls into the Navel, forcing Zoro and the Devil Pirates to rescue Luffy and Samuel after they fall into water. Zoro and Gin locate a village, where they meet Hamu and Meroie, who give food to the group. Once he awakens, Luffy reveals he and his group are pirates. After Hamu leaves, the pirates learn about the village from Meroie. When she asks, the pirates agree to save the village and help Hamu avenge his mother.

Captain Joke

Luffy leads the crews towards a mountain. As they arrive at the base, a monster appears, and Zoro stays behind to fight it. As the rest continue to head up the mountain, Sanji arrives to help defeat the monster. At the Top of the Mountain, Luffy and Samuel fight a second monster while Gin leads the rest of the crews, including Nami and Usopp into the mountain.

In the mountain, Gin and the remaining Straw Hats fight against Joke and the final monster. Gin, Pearl and Ideaman manage to defeat the final monster while the Straw Hats help Hamu kill Joke.

As the crew reunite, the are disappointed by the lack of treasure, but glad that they could assist Hamu.
