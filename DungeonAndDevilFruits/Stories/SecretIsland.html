Secret Island

The Secret Island Arc is the third arc of the Water 7 Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Omatsuri Island

Crew arrive on the island after getting a message in a bottle. Arrive at Island. Luffy agrees to the Trial of Hell.

Disappearing Crew

Samuel is the first to disappear, then Charles, followed by Johnny and Yosaku.

Lily Carnation

Once all crew members have disappeared, Luffy defeats Lily Carnation.

Story Impact

  • Luffy gets a Very Rare Surge, and unlocks Gear Second
