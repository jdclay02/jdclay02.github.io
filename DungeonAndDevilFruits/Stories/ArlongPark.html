Arlong Park

The Arlong Park Arc is the sixth arc of the East Blue Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Pursuit

Luffy's group head to Cocoyashi, and Samuel explains the Warlords to him. Meanwhile, Nami meets with Arlong. At the same time, Zoro's group arrive on Coconami. Zoro's group locates the Merry, and find themselves in the ruins of Gosa village, where Zoro is captured by Fishmen. Usopp, Kaya, Charles and Johnny escape and meet Nojiko.

Luffy's group encounter Moomoo, who gives them a ride after Luffy scares it. At Arlong Park, Zoro and Nami have a conversation with Observation Haki. Zoro fakes Nami killing him, and escapes. At Arlong Park, Usopp saves Genzo.

Tragedy of a Navigator

Luffy group arrives at Cocoyashi, and reunite with the Straw Hats. Nojiko explains her and Nami's story. Nezumi arrives, and takes Nami's saved up money. Nami goes to confront Arlong, who laughs at her.

Nami asks for Luffy's Help. Walk to Arlong Park.

Straw Hat Pirates vs Arlong Pirates

Luffy vs Arlong, Zoro vs Hatchan, Sanji vs Kuroobi, Usopp vs Chew, Samuel vs Momoo, Charles vs Pisaro, Johnny vs Kaneshiro, Yosaku vs Take, Kaya vs Shioyake. Fall of Arlong Park.

Marines under the leadership of Nezumi arrive. They are defeated.

Party at Cocoyashi, the crew recruit Nojiko. The crew leave Arlong park the next day.

At sea, the crew recieve a newspaper, it contains bounties for the crew.

  • "Strawhat" Monkey D. Luffy - $45,000,000
  • "Dark Forest" Avery D. Samuel = $25,000,000
  • "Devil Man" Gin = $27,000,000
  • "Iron Wall" Pearl = #13,000,000

Story Impact

  • Crew Experiances 2 Spirit Surges, Except Nami who gets 1
  • Nojiko is recruited as a Botanist
