Island of Rare Animals

The Island of Rare Animals Arc is the first arc in the East Blue Saga of Dungeon and Devil Fruits, it introduces the main characters, Samuel and Charles Avery as well as the Straw Hat Pirates.

Summary

The Brother's Arrival

Brothers Samuel and Charles Avery awaken on a mysterious island, with Charles blaming Samuel for their situation. There is a flashback showing how they got to the island, Samuel met an Isekai Fairy who sent the two Brothers to the Island of Rare Animals in the East Blue after Sam rolled on several tables. It is revealed that Sam chose to bring along Charles due to him being dissatisfied with his life.

After their argument, the two brothers take stock of their situation, and it is revealed that Samuel has an instructive guide to haki written by Silvers Rayleigh, while Charles has a devil fruit. After Sam explains how devil fruits work, Charles decides that he doesn't want it, as losing the abilitiy to swim in a world of water seems to be a bad idea. Samuel finds the additional power gained from a devil fruit an acceptable trade, and consumes it with Charles's permission. It is revealed to be the Mori Mori no Mi, the Forest Logia that was origionally consumed by Aramaki.

After taking stock of their situation, the brothers team up to explore the Island. While exploring, they encounter a grove of fruit tree's, and are attacked by Gaimon while collecting some of the fruit. The bullet passses through Samuel, and it is revealed that he possesses Observation Haki. The brother's reveal Gaimon's location, and talk him down.

The Pirates Arrive

A few days after arriving on the island, Samuel and Charles are sparing. Samuel is practicing using his logia abilities, while Charles is practicing using Armament to coat his weapon and strike Logia's. While they are sparing, Gaimon rushes over and informs the two that Pirates have arrived.

Samuel, Charles and Gaimon watch the pirates disembark, and overhear the captain loudly complaining about the broken keel on the ship. The First Mate brings up their current location, and about the supposed treasure. The captain gives orders to find the treasure and kill all who stand in their way.

The first mate leads half the crew, five men into the forest. Gaimon runs his guardian of the forest routine, but the pirates are unaffected. Samuel drags one of the pirates into the forest and kills him with his logia powers, freaking out the pirates. Samuel entangles the pirates, allowing Charles to suprise them, and defeat a few more. Samuel drags off another pirate, leaving just the First Mate, who is on the verge of a mental breakdown. Charles reveals himself, and reveals his Conqueror's Haki, and terrifies the first mate, who tries to run, but is killed by Samuel.

Samuel, Charles and Gaimon return to be beach and watch the pirates continue to unload their ship. Gaimon tries his guardian of the forest act again, and gets a bullet shot at him in responce. Sam entangles the pirates while Charles charges at them. The duo take advantage of their suprise attack to take out two of the pirates, while the others are distracted. As the remaining pirates start fighting against the brothers, Charles fights the crew, while Samuel enters his Elemental Form and fights against the captain.

Charles is able to take out the three crew members he is fighting against by overpowering their strikes with his haki-infused cutlass, while their captain is frustrated with Samuel's elemental form being immune to his attacks. When the Captain threatens to burn down the island, he is killed by Samuel.

The Straw Hat Pirates

About a week after the attack of the pirates, the island trio have fully explored and looted the ship they came on, and found that it was irreparable. In the meantime, the brothers have kept up their training, with Charles now fully understanding how to user weapon hardening, and Samuel being able to better control his plants.

Gaimon inform the brothers about two ships which are approaching the island, and the brothers join him in watching them approach. Samuel is able to instantly recognize Luffy and Nami on the ship. As the duo approach, Gaimon runs his Guardian of the Forest routine, which the two Straw Hats are unimpressed by. Gaimon attempts to shoot Luffy, but it fails due to his rubber body. Before things can escalate further, Samuel reveals himself and starts talking to Luffy and Nami, offering food.

The island trio and the Straw Hats eat lunch together, and Samuel offer to play a game with Luffy to win free passage to Loguetown. Luffy agrees. The game is to guess something about the other person, if you are right, they drink and you make another guess, if you are wrong, you drink, and switch questioner. Luffy begins:

  • You have a Devil Fruit: Correct
  • Your Devil Fruit is a Paramecia: Wrong
  • You are the youngest of three: Correct
  • You have Conqueror's Haki: Debate, question dismissed due to being unknown
  • You have the Hito Hito no Mi, Model Nika: Debate, question dismissed due to disagreement
  • Both your brothers are alive: Debate, Dismissed due to disagreement about Sabo being alive
  • Your father is the leader of the revolutionary army: Debate, is correct
  • You are asexual: Correct

Samuel wins free passage for himself and his brother. Luffy want's to play again, due to only being able to ask two questions. Samuel agrees, they are playing for Sam and Charles to join the crew. Samuel starts.

  • Your dream is to find the One Piece: Wrong
  • Your Devil fruit is a Logia: Correct
  • Your brother is your best friend: Wrong
  • You got your Straw Hat from Red Haired Shanks: Correct
  • Your least favorite food is Cherry Pie: Wrong
  • Your favorite color is Green: Correct, but to simple
  • Your Devil Fruit is Plant Based: Correct, the Mori Mori no Mi
  • You have Conqueror's Haki: Correct

Due to Luffy winning, Samuel agrees to join the crew, and Charles asks Luffy if he can join as well, which he accepts. With lunch done, Samuel and Charles load supplies onto Nami's boat, and board Luffy's raft as they set off.
