Jaya

The Jaya Arc is the fifth arc of the Sky Island Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Ship from the Sky

The Straw Hats are sailing when they notice that the Log Pose has been captured by a Sky Island. A ship crashes down next to the crew, and while exploring it the crew run into and befriend Masira. When a giant sea turtle attacks, the Commanders manage to defeat it, but the crew are scared off by giant shadow creatures. Robin leads the crew to Jaya using a Eternal Pose from Masira's ship.

Dreamless City

The crew pull up their ship, and dock at Mock Town. In Town, the crew splits up, with one staying with the ship, one getting supplies while the final group goes to search for information. While Samuel's group picks up lost of food, specifically funeral fruit baskets. Luffy's group encounters the Blackbeard pirates, as well as Bellamy while asking a bartender about Sky Island.

Luffy's group are laughed out of the bar, and are reassured by Blackbeard. As the Straw Hats leave to meet with Cricket, Van Augur informs Blackbeard of Luffy's bounty. The crew meet Cricket, and collect a South Bird after forming an alliance.

While in town, Bellamy attacks Luffy again, and is defeated in one punch.

Back at Cricket's, the crew load up on the modified Merry, and invite Cricket to join them.

World on the Move

Shanks fails to arrange a meeting with Whitebeard. Laffitte offers Blackbeard for the position of Warlord.

Story Impact

  • The Crew gets a Rare Spirit Surge
  • The Crew allies with the Saruyama Alliance
