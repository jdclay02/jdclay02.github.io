G-8

The G-8 Arc is the seventh and final arc of the Sky Island Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

Arrival

The Straw Hats land in G-8 Marine Base, and scatter. Hiding amongst the Marines.

Chaos

The Straw Hats cause chaos while trying to escape.

Escape

The Straw Hats escape through the Jaws of Navarone.
