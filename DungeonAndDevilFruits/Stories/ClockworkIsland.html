Clockwork Island

The Clockwork Island Arc is the ninth arc in the East Blue Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Missing Merry

The Straw Hat's are relaxing on a beach while the Devil Pirates pick up supplies, as they were unable to at Loguetown. While relaxing, Samuel and Sanji notice Johnny and Yosaku falling unconsious, and alert the crew when they notice the Going Merry sailing away. The crew rush to the Devil Pirates ship, and take off with the Pirates after the Merry.

The Trump Siblings

The two crews chase after the Merry, and come across a fleet of seven ships, led by Honey Queen and Boo Jack. Luffy, Samuel, Sanji and Usopp board the ship, and ask about their missing ship, which Honey Queen admits to having had taken, annoying Luffy who demands it back. When she refuses, Sanji takes out Boo Jack, Samuel transforms and starts destroying the ship. Usopp uses the opportunity to burn down the sails of the rest of the ships while Luffy punches out Honey Queen.

Using a map in the possession of the siblings, the crews head to Clockwork Island.

Clockwork Island

At the base of the island, the crews meet up with Borodo and Akisu, the thief brothers. They form a temporary alliance after they inform the Straw Hats that the Going Merry was taken to the top of the island. The group climb the staircase, with Samuel daming the water with his plants, while Sanji and Zoro kick and slice the boulder's apart, terrifying the Thief Brothers.

As they reach the top of the tower, they head to the town to get some food to recharge, and learn of the town's predicament. When asked to flee for their safety, Luffy refuses.

At the Trump Castle, The Strawhat parasail in, while the Devil Pirates lead a ground assault. While Luffy heads for Bear King, Zoro comes into contact with Pin Joker, while Usopp leads Kaya to a confrontation with Skunk One. Nami and Nojiko fight the ship thieves, the Hyena three while Sanji and Samuel lead an assault on the rest of the Trump Pirates Forces.

During the fighting, the Diamon Clock was destroyed by Luffy and Bear King, leading the Island falling down. As this happens, the Going Merry is outfited with a Sail, allowing the Straw Hat Pirates, the Devil Pirates, the Thief Brothers and the Civilians to escape.
