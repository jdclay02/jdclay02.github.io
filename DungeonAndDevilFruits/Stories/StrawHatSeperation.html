Straw Hat Seperation

The Straw Hat Seperation Arc is the second arc of the Timeskip Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Captain and his Commanders

Luffy and Rayleigh are dropped off on Rusukaina. Zoro begs Mihawk for Training. Samuel arrives on Baltigo, recieves a newspaper with the new bounties of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet. Sanji argues with Ivankov.

The Senior Officer

Nami studies weather under Haredas. Charles spars against Humandrills. Usopp and Kaya work out with Haracles. Chopper and Nojiko study plants. Robin arrives on Baltigo, and is greeted by Samuel. Franky studies Vegapunk's blueprints. Brook perfroms his music.

The Crew

Johnny and Yosaku guard the Thousand Sunny. Vivi glares at Crocodile in Alabasta. Conis dodges an attack from Nala.
