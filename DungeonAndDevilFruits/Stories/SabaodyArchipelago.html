Sabaody Archipelago

The Sabaody Archipelago Arc is the First arc of the Summit War Saga of Dungeons and Devil Fruits.

Summary

The Halfway Mark

Samuel explains Fishman Island and the Red Line to the crew. The crew meet Camie, and agree to help rescue her friend. The crew defeat the Flying Fish Riders, and rescue Hatchan. Hatchan apologizes to Nami. The crew defeat Duval, and befriend him.

Island of Bubbles

Samuel sits the crew down to discuss their future plans. Samuel explains the origional timeline, and just as he finishes ,they get a call from Marco, informing them of Ace's Execution Date, and asking for there assistance, which Luffy agrees to. Samuel explains the timeskip, which Luffy decides that if they get separated, they'll train for 2 years, but if they don't, they won't.

Arrive on Sabaody, island tour. See Rosward and Shalria. The group arrive to Shakky's Rip Off Bar, and meet Shakuyaku, who Samuel recognized and informs the crew about. Shakky shows the crew their new bounties, and explains the 20 supernovas.

The Crew leaves the Bar, and split up. Vivi and Nami find Bonney, and hit it off. Those three later save Zoro from a World Noble. The crew learn of Camie's kidnapping, and use Observation Haki to track them. The Crew arrive at the Human Action House. Samuel takes a seat between Kid and Law.

After failing to buy Camie, Nami is panicing when Charlos try's to shoot Hatchan. Luffy blocks the shot. When Charlos tries to do it again, Luffy punches out Charlos. While the room in Panicing, Samuel brags about his captain, while Zoro knocks out Shalria, who was going to shoot Camie. Sanji also knocks out Roswald before he can use his Den Den Mushi to call anyone. The Crew meet and leave with Rayleigh.

When the Marines try and attack the crew, they are knocked out by Conquerors Haki.
