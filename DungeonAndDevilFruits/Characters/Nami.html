    Description

    Appearance

    Nami is a slim young woman standing at 5'7" with orange hair and brown eyes. Many consider her to be attractive. She has the most fluid fashion of the Straw Hats, changing her hair style and clothes often.

    Personality

    Nami is a very intelligent woman who, while not a pacifist, dislikes killing. While greedy, Nami has a soft spot for children and her friends and will go out of her way to help and protect them.

    Biography

    Background

    Nami is an Orphan from the Oykat Kingdom, and was found as a baby by a Marine named Bell-mere. Bell-mere took Nami and her new sister Nojiko to Cocoyashi Village and adopted the two. When she was young, her adoptive mother was killed by Arlong, and Nami sold herself into Slavery to try and purchase the Village.

    Prior to the series, she was recruited by Luffy after he defeated Buggy in Orange town.

    Relationships

    Luffy

    Nami respects Luffy as her captain, bute gets angry at his childishness. This often manifests and violent outbreaks. Despite this, the two care for and trust each other.

    Zoro

    Zoro and Nami often bicker over small and petty things. Despite this, Nami trusts Zoro's fighting skills. The two are drinking buddies.
