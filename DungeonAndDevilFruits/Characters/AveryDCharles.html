    Description

    Appearance

    Charles is fit young man standing at 5'8" with blond hair and blue eyes. His wardrobe leans towards blues and white, which often gets him mistaken for a Marine.

    Personality

    Charles is usually a kind and cheerful man, however when he is in a bad mood, or is having anxiety about a situation, his personality switches to become vulgar and belittling or morose.

    Biography

    Background

    Origionally from Earth, Charles was a college dropout and current employee at an electronics store.

    He was brought to the world of one piece alongside his bother after said brother was visited by the Isekai Fairy.

    Relationships

    Samuel

    Charles did not get along with his brother for a long time. Their interactions were usually mean-spirited, with Charles belittling Samuel for his intrests. While Sam was away at university, both of them mellowed out, and when Sam returned, the two started getting along again.
