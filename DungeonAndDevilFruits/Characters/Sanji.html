    Description

    Appearance

    Sanji is a lean young man standing at 5'10" with blond hair and brown eyes. He prefers to wear light suits in black.

    Personality

    Sanji is calm, cool and collected, and is compared to a secret agent.

    Biography

    Background

    Sanji was born as a prince in the Germa Kingdom to it's king, Judge and his queen Sora. Growing up, he was close to his Mother and Older Sister, due to his father constantly berating him and his brother's bullying him.
