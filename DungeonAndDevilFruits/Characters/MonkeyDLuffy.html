Description

Appearance

Luffy is a young man, standing at 172cm with black hair, black eyes, and a slim, muscilar build. He is primarily know for the Straw Hat which his Epithet is derived from. He prefers to wear thin red shirts, blue shorts and sandals. He has a scar under his left eye.

Personality

Luffy is both carefree and fearless, and has an unwarvering belief in himself. He is relativly simple minded, not bothering with nuiance unless it concerns a member of his crew or family.

When his crew or family are threatened, Luffy becomes extremely serious.

Biography

Background

Luffy was born in Foosha Village on Dawn Island to Monkey D. Dragon and an unknown Woman. When he was six, he met and befriended the Red Hair Pirates, and Shanks in particular. When he was seven he met Ace and Sabo, and became sworn brothers with them.

Prior to the series, he befriended Koby and defeated Alvida. In Shells Town, he defeated Axe-Hand Morgan and recruited Zoro. In Orange town, he defeated Buggy and recruited Nami.

Relationships

Zoro

Luffy's first crew member, and a former bounty hunter. The two share a mutual respect for each other and their skills. He was initially recruited in Shells town where Zoro offered to join his crew after Luffy freed him, which Luffy would have done anyways.

Nami

Luffy's second crew member, and (secretly) a member of the Arlong Pirates. Luffy respects Nami's skills at Navigation and relies on her. While the two argue alot when this are serious, they both can rely on each other.
