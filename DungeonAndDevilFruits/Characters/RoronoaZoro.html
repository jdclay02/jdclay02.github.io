    Description

    Appearance

    Zoro is a muscular man starting at 5'10" with tanned skin. He has green hair and black eyes. He is always seen with three swords at his hip, and while two of them change as they are broken and replaces, he always has the Wado Ichimonji.

    Personality

    Zoro has a stern, serious demenor. He has a strong sense of Honor, and is very loyal to those who gain his trust. Because of this loyalty, he is slow to give people his trust. Despite is rather distant persona, he has a kind heart.

    Biography

    Background

    Zoro was born in Shimotsuki Village to Roronoa Arashi and Tera. As a child, he was friends with and trained with Kuina. After her death, he recieved the Wado Ichimonji from her father to carry on their shared dream.

    Prior to the series, he was imprisoned by the marines of Shells Town and was both rescued and recruited by Luffy. The duo went to orange town and recruited Nami and defeated Buggy the Clown.

    Relationships

    Luffy

    Zoro trust Luffy implicitly, and will follow his decisions reguardless of the consequences. Despite this, he will turn on Luffy is he gets in the way of his dream.

    Nami

    Zoro and Nami often bicker over small and petty things. While Zoro speaks of how he does not care about Nami, the two are drinking buddies.
