    Description

    Appearance

    Yosaku is an average size man in his twenties standing at 6'1". He has black hair, and and black eyes.

    Personality

    Yosaku is happy-go-lucky, much like Johnny. He tends to be the more laid back of the pair. He is loyal to Johnny, his mentor Zoro and his captain, Luffy.

    Biography

    Background

    Yosaku became a Bounty Hunter at the age of 16, after witnessing bounty hunters save his village from bandits when he was 8. He formed a team with Johnny two years later, and was mentored briefly by Zoro three years after that.

    Relationships
