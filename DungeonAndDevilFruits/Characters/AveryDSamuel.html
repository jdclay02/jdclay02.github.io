    Description

    Appearance

    Samuel is a thin young man standing at 5'9". He was brown hair and green eyes, and wears a wardrobe that leans towards greens and browns. His wardrobe choice preempted his aquisition of the Mori Mori no Mi.

    Personality

    Samuel outwardly appears to be calm and kind, this is a mask to his natural sarcasm and anxiety. His anxiety has only gotten worse after he accidentally pulled his brother into another world.

    Biography

    Background

    Origionally from Earth, Samuel was a recent college graduate and current software developer for a cybersecurity firm.

    Samuel was visited by the Isekai Fairy shortly after moving out after college. During his meeting with her, he chose to bring his younger brother with him, due to a conversation they had earlier about Charles being unsatisfied with his life.

    Relationships

    Charles

    Sam cares greatly about his brother, and will do just about anything for him. This was not always true, as before Sam left for college, the two did not get along, and constantly got on each other's nerves. Their adventures after being isekai'ed have brought the brothers closer together.
