Description

Appearance

Usopp is a lean young man standing at 5'9" with a brown afro, brown eyes and tanned skin. He prefers to wear utilitarian clothes in brown.

Personality

Usopp is a cowardly liar, however in spite of this, he is selfless, compassionate and strives to become a brave warrior of the sea. He enjoys telling tall tales.

Biography

Background

Usopp was born in Syrup Village on the Gecko Islands to Yasopp and Banchina. When he was young, his mother passed away, and he become close to Kaya. While is mother was alive, he would pretend pirates were coming to try and cheer her up. This would continue as he got older, as a form of rememberance.

Relationships

Luffy

Usopp was greatly inspired by Luffy, and gets along well with him due to their shared childish personality. The two often goof off together.

Zoro

Usopp respects Zoro's strength, and they share a stong bond as crewmates. Despite this, Usopp is scared of Zoro due to his fearsome power.

Nami

Usopp gets along with Nami, but his is often intimidated by her violet behavior. During the East Blue Saga, they were seen (Along with Kaya) to be the only normal members of the crew.

Samuel

Usopp gets along with Samuel, and respects both his strength and his intelligence. Usopp views Samuel as a favored Uncle, due to Samuel taking the time to teach him Observation Haki.

Charles

Usopp and Charles get along very well, as they share a similar childish personality.

Kaya

Usopp's childhood best friend and crush. The two share a mutual fondness for each other, striving to improve for each other's sake.
