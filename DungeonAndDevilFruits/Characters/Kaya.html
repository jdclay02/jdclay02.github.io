Description

Appearance

Kaya is a thin young woman standing at 5'6" with blonde hair and blue eyes. While she was being poisoned, she wore mostly white, but now that she's clear, she's switched to blacks.

Personality

Kaya is a caring young woman with an aversion to violence. Now that she is healthy, she is carefree, and trusting of her crew.

Biography

Background

Kaya was born in Syrup Village on the Gecko Islands to a merchant family. When she was young, her parents passed away, and she become close to Usopp. During this time, she came mostly under the care of Klahadore.
