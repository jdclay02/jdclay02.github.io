    Description

    Appearance

    Baern is a 4 foot 4 inch Gold Dwarf. He has dark red hair with a well kept white beard, and grey eyes. He wardrobe is practicle, and leans towards browns and greys.

    Personality

    Baern is a polite and respectful cleric, however his doubts about his faith lead him to be hesitent to use his divine magic. These doubts also lead to him not trusting himself and following others over his own desires.
