Description

Appearance

Callie is a 3'1" young woman with brown hair and brown eyes. She prefers to wear non-descript, durable close in dark colors, mainly brown and black

Personality

Callie is both proud and reckless, with a devil may care attitide in most situations. This attidute is only pre-empted by her loyaty and care for her friends and family.

Biography

Background

Callie grew up in Phandalin with her aunt. When she was a teenager, she joined the Redbrands and ran with them for a few years before being kicked out and nearly assassinated.

Relationships

Iarno Abreck / Glasstaff

Callie's former lover, he sent assassins after her to prevent their relationship from speading to the other members of the Gang.

Halia Thornton

Callie's criminal contact in Phandalin.
