Description

Appearance

Sariel is a 5 foot 1 inch young woman with copper hair and silver eyes. She tends to wear priestly clothes in Greys.

Personality

Sariel is a very intelligent woman with little real work experiance. Her desire for knowledge, irreguardless of type, is an addiction.

Biography

Background

Born to a prostitute, Sariel's name was misrecorded in the orphanage ledger, leading to her incorrect name. She was sold to the Temple of Oghma at the age of 15, where she became an acolyte.
