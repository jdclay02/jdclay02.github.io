    Description

    Appearance

    Darvin is a 6 foot, 3 inch young adult human with dark brown hair and brown eyes. He leans towards formal, durable clothes in red, white and yellow.

    Personality

    Darvin as a polite, responsible young noble with a strong desire for gold. Due to his upbringing, he desires to reclaim his families ancestral land, or gain new lands for himself and his familt

    Biography

    Background

    Born to two displaced noble parents, Darvin grew up in Neverwinter as was constantly told of his families noble origins.

    Relationships

    Count and Countess Corlinn

    Darvin had a stong relationship with his parents while he was young, however it had faded due to their insistance and constant waxing about their families ancestral landes. By the time of his fathers death, they only saw each other for holidays and at city or noble fuctions.
