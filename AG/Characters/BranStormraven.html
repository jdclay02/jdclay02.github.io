    Bran Stormraven is an True Neutral Human Wizard from Earth.

    Matthew, now known as Bran, is a human originally from Earth. He was brough to Toril through the Magic Release of the Second Sundering. He took the name of one of his Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition chracters upon being brough into the world, after discovering that he possessed the abilities of a Wizard.

    Description

    Appearance

    Bran Stormraven is a 5 feet, 11 inch tall pale human with charcoal black hair and storm grey eyes. He tends towards simple, durable clothes with darker colors.

    While traveling, he keeps his spellbook in a sachel slung across his body and over his right shoulder. His component pouch is attached to his belt on his right hip.

    Personality

    Bran appears to be a calm and composed person with a sarcastic sense of humor, which was origionally a defensive measure to prevent himself from panicking after discovering he had been sent to Toril.

    This mask slips when around people he trusts revealing his true personality, which is more friendly and excitable while still maintaining his sarcastic wit.

    Biography

    Background

    While origionally a Software Engineer from Earth, Bran has taken up the role of a Wizard from Neverwinter after being brought to Toril. Due to it's close proximity, Bran assumes the cause of his summoning to be the Sundering, which occured the year previous.
