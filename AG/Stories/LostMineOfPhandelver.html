Lost Mine of Phandelver

The Lost Mine of Phandelver is the first Adventure in 1485 of The Adventurers Guild, and it introduces the Adventurers Guild. This Adventure introduces the main character, Bran Stormraven. The main base of the Guild in Phandalin is also introduced, as well as the future Guildhall. Summary, Summary.

Summary

On the 2nd of Hammer, 1485, Bran Stormraven meets with Gundren Rockseeker and Sildar Hallwinter, as well as the four others that they hired in Neverwinter. Gundren gives the five a job to deliver mining supplies to Phandalin. Unknown to the other four, one of the hired guards Baern is the cousin and secretly a spy planted by Gundren to keep an eye on the others.

Goblin Arrows

On the 5th of Hammer, the group are ambushed by Goblins, but manage to defeat them. They even manange to capture one of them. Their interrogation of the captured goblin leads them to the base of the Goblin Ambushers. The follow down the trail, and manage to avoid the traps.

When the party arrive at the Cragmaw Hideout, the tables are turned, and the party become the ambushers as they take out the guards at the entry. In the hideout, they free the wolves, and take out some more guards before locating Klarg, the leader of the Hideout. Through their collected treasure, they lean that the hideout Goblins swear fealty to the King at Cragmaw Castle. After looking over the Treasure Hoard, they continue exploring the hideout and locate the Goblin underboss, Yeemik. Bran bluffs, which gives the party the oppertunity to take out the other Goblins and rescue Sildar.

That night, after bringing the Lionshield Coaster goods back to the Rockseeker goods. Sildar informs the party about how he and Gundren were captured. He also give the party more information on why they are transporting goods.

Phandalin and the Redbrands

On the 6th of Hammer, the party and Sildar arrive in Phandalin. With Sildar, they go to the Stonehill inn and are given rooms due to Sildar paying for them. After eating lunch and listening to rumors, Sildar leaves the group to visit the town master, while the party go to Barthen's provisions to offload the Rockseeker brother's goods, and learn of Thardin and Nundro Rockseeker going missing. Afterwards, they go to the Lionshield Coster and turn in the Goods the group found at the Cragmaw Hideout.

The party split up to track down Rumors. Bran joins Sariel in visiting Sister Garaele and learning of Agatha the Banshee. Meanwhile, Baern meets with Daran Edermath to learn about the the person digging around Old Owl Well. At the same time, Callie visits Halia Thornton, and gets a mission to assassinate her former lover Glasstaff. Afterwards, she visits her Aunt and cousin Carp to learn about Uncle Reidoth and the secret entrance to the Redbrand base. While this is happening, Darvin meets back up with Sildar at the Town master's office. While there, he agrees to deal with the Orcs for Harbin, and locate Iarno for Sildar. After the party meet back up, they encounter Redbrands, who recognize Callie. After defeating the Redbrands, one makes a run for Tresendar Manor, accidentally leaning the party to the entrance.

At the request of Darvin, Baern and Callie, Sariel and Bran join the group in entering the Redbrand Hideout. Heading down into the cellar, they knock out the guards in the Barracks, and tie them up with their cloaks. Investigating the rooms, Bran locates Iarno's getaway pack which he remembers from being a DM for the Adventure. Passing though a trapped hallway and into a Crypt where the Party destroy some skeletons. Continuing into the Slave Pens, the Party defeats some more Redbrands and rescue the three surviving Dendrar's, Mirna the mother, Nilsa the daughter and Nars the son. Leading the group out, they pass an Armory as well as a Storeroom and a Work Area before reaching a Crevasse. In the Crevasse, the party fights a Nothic, after refusing to negtiate with the aberration and the Dendrar's are subjected to seeing the Nothic consuming their Father.

After escorting the Dendrar's out via the secret entrance, the party continues to explore the Redbrand Base. In the Crevasse they locate a chest of magic items, gems and coin. Within the chest is Talon, the magic ancestral sword of the Tresendar Family. Continuing into the hideout, the party locate a guard barracks and slay the bugbears who are harrassing a Goblin. After interrogating the goblin, the party decide to leave Droop alive. In the common room, the party capture more Redbrands, and take the wager of their dice game. Before entering Glasstaff's chambers, Callie reveals that she knows a secret entrance into the Wizard's study, and suggests a pinsir manuvere which the party accepts. Callie and Sariel wait in the secret entrance while Darvin, Baern and Bran go through the Workshop and find that Callie and Sariel have already killed Glasstaff.

After collecting the treasure in the Wizard's Workshop and Glasstaff's quarters, they return the captured Redbrands and the body of Glasstaff to Sildar and Harbin. Sildar identified Glasstaff as Iarno, and pays the party. While the rest of the party returns to the inn to rest, Callie and Darvin stick around town. Darvin meets with Sildar in secret, and is inducted into the Lord's Alliance as a Cloak. Meanwhile, Callie meets with Halia, and is brought into the Zhentarim as a Fang.

The Spider's Web

On the morning of the 7th of Hammer, the party sets out from Phandalin towards Conyberry, from which they can go to Agatha's Lair, Old Owl Well and the Wyvern Tor. That night, they are attacked by Stirges. The next day, they are attacked by Goblins. On the 9th of Hammer, they arrive in Conyberry, where they slay an Ogre. Later that Day, they go to Agatha's and complete Sister Garaele's request to learn the location of Bowgentle's spellbook.

That night, they rest in Conyberry and are attacked by Orcs. The next day, the 10th of Hammer, they head to Old Owl Well and meet the necromancer Hamun Kost. None of the party are okay with necromancy, and they end up killing Hamun and his undead minions. From there, the party to to the Wyvern Tor and destroy the Orcs. The next day, while they are on their way back to Conyberry, they are attacked by wolves, and once in Conyberry, they are attacked by Ghouls.

Seting out the next day, 12th of Hammer, the party are attacked by an Owlbear. The next daym they are attacked by Hobgoblins. This turns out to be a good think, and the party are able to capture one of them, and use them as a Guide to Cragmaw Castle. Using their guide, they arrive at Nightfall.

Approching from the south, Callie is able to pick open the the Postern Gate. The Party clear the Hobgoblin guard barracks and the Hobgoblins guarding king Grol before bursting into the King's Quarters. The Party slay the king and the apparent Drow messenger, which is revealed to be a doppleganger after death. After defeating the King and the messenger, the party search the room while Baern heals his cousin. Once Gundren is awake, they learn more about his capture, and what the Black Spider wants.

With Gundren rescued, they set about Sariel's objective, cleansing the Shrine located in the castle. As part of their preperations, they slay the remaining members of the Cragmaw Tribe and collect their treasure. During this time, they locate an Owlbear that the Cragmaw Tribe were trying, and failing to tame. The monster is but down, along with the Grick owned by the Goblin. Overnight, Sariel reconcecrates the Shrine to Oghma with Baern's assistance.

The next day, as the party are leaving the castle to return to Phandalin, a Hobgoblin war band return, and engage the party. The party defeat the Warband, and leave unscathed.

Wave Echo Cave

Two days later, on the 15th of Hammer, the party return to Phandalin. They meet with Sildar, and make plans to set out for Wave Echo cave the next day at the urging of Gundren. In the meantime, the party split up to turn in their individual quests, with Baern meeting with Daran to join the Order of the Gauntlet as a Chevall. Bran and Sariel meet with Sister Garaele and both join the Harpers as Watchers.

Later in the day, the party head out for Wave Echo Cave, arriving near nightfall. They create a camp at the cave entrance, and host a funeral for Tharden Rockseeker, who's corpse they found. After resting, the party traveled through the old entrance, figting of Stirges before clearing out the Old Guardroom and tha Assayers' Office. From these, the party passed through a fungal cavern before arriving at a Starry Cavern that led them to the quarters of Mormesk the Wraith, who tasked the party with slaying the Spectator in the Forge before returning for their reward.

Once their dealing with Mormesk was complete, the party sleared out the smelter cavern, dealing with a Flameskull along the way. From there, the party went and cleared out the mess hall, before scavenging the Storeroom and clearing the south barracks. Using the barracks as a launching point, the party went and cleared the mine tunnels before coming across a pool of dark water that held a wand of magic missiles. After looting the pool's skeleton, the party cleared out the North barracks, which contained the reinforcements of the Black Spider.

With most of the cave clear, the party converged on the Black Spider's base of operations. They first took out the remaining doppleganger and the bugbear work crew (locating Gauntlet's of Ogre Power in the process) before sneaking into the priests quarters and rescuing Nundro, the third Rockseeker brother. With Nundro secured, the party entered the Temple of Dumathoin. Within the Temple, the party fought against Nezznar the Black Spider and his Giant Spider guards and slew them.
