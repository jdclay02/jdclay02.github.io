Archimedes Johannson

Archimedes Johannson, also known as Archie is the Navigator of the New Game Pirates, as well as their Sniper. His Dream is to visit every island in the world, he has a secondary dream to defeat the man who killed his old crew.

Born in the Briss Kingdom in the South Blue, he was hired as an assistant Navigator on a pirate crew when he was nineteen. This crew was at first defeated by a Marine Captain when he was twenty one when they attempted to head into the grand line. Three years later that Captain, now a Commodore, destroyed Archies old crew, and captured the Captain and Primary Navigator. He spendt the Next year in Hiding

Appearance

Archie had naturally blond hair, which he dyes a bright green in memory of his old captain who had naturally green hair. He prefers to wear a three piece suit, whith a longer coat over it, and a black hat.

Personality

Archie is a cautions and patient individual who prefers to stay in the back during fights, and can come off as being callous to others. He also enjoys spending time talking with interesting people, and listening to his crew. He holds great loyalty to his current and old crews, he held a great deal of Respect for his old captains, Maxwell and Keep Frip. He is very loyal to his current Captain, Chad. Archie has respect for people who think for themselves, and dislikes when people follow orders they disagree with without questioning them.

Archie can come off as callous to people who don't know him, as he doesn't have a problem killing people who fight against the crew, and refuse to surrender.

Physical Abilities

Strength

Archie posseses slightly below average physical strength.

Speed and Agility

Archie is a very fast and agile fighter, being the fastest sprinter on the crew, and being able to dodge most attacks without much difficulties.

Durability

Despite rarely being hit, Archie is suprisingly durable.

Devil Fruit

Archie ate the Nagi Nagi no Mi during the Coliseum Island Arc, and gained the ability to calm vibrations to silence himself. He primarily uses the ability to silence the area around himself to quiet his gun, and improve his stealth.

Archie also has another technique Sound Tunnel which he uses when he wants to have a private conversation with another person by sculpting his calmed area to only include his and the person he wishes to speak to's head

Marksmanship

Archie is a skilled marksman, being able to drop lower ranked marines with a single shot.

Explosive

Archie has an affinity for blowing things up as distractions, as demonstrated with the Bakery and the Weapons Factory.

History

Past

Archie was born in the Briss Kingdom, where he lived until he was nineteen. While living in the kingdom, he studies at the Naval Academy until he met his first captain, Maxwell, and joined him in being a pirate.

When Archie was twenty-one, the crew made their first attempt to enter the grand line, where they were beaten down by a marine captain, however they managed to escape with no casualties.

When Archie was Twenty-Four, his crew attempted to raid a Marine Base, hoping to take revenge on the Marine Captain. They failed, and the Captain who origionally defeated them had been promotted to Commodore, before he destroyed Archies old crew, and imprisoned Maxwell and the Navigator.
