Archimedes Johannson

Archimedes Johannson, also known as Archie is the Navigator of the New Game Pirates, as well as their Sniper. His Dream is to visit every island in the world, and he also has a secondary dream to defeat the man who killed his old crew.

Born in the Briss Kingdom in the South Blue, he was hired as an assistant Navigator on a pirate crew when he was nineteen. This crew was first defeated by a Marine Captain when he was twenty one and they attempted to head into the Grand Line. Three years later that same Captain, now a Commodore, destroyed Archies old crew, and captured the Captain and Primary Navigator. Archie spent the next year in hiding

Appearance

Archie had naturally blond hair, which he dyes a bright green in memory of his old captain who had naturally green hair. He prefers to wear a three piece suit, with a longer coat over it, and a black hat.

Personality

Archie is a cautions and patient individual who prefers to stay in the back during fights, and can come off as being callous to others. He enjoys spending time talking with interesting people, and listening to his crew. He holds great loyalty to his current and former crews, he held a great deal of Respect for his old captains, Maxwell and Keek Frip. He is very loyal to his current Captain, Chad. Archie has respect for people who think for themselves and dislikes it when people follow orders they disagree with without questioning them.

Archie can come off as callous to people who don't know him, as he doesn't have a problem with killing people who fight against the crew, however, he wont kill someone who surrenders.

Abilities and Powers

Archie as a versatile skill set that allows him to perform a variety of tasks on the ship.

Physical Abilities

Strength

Archie posseses slightly below average physical strength, expecially when compared to other members of the crew.

Speed and Agility

Archie is a very fast and agile fighter, being one of the fastest sprinters on the crew, and also being able to dodge most attacks without much difficulty.

Durability

Despite rarely being hit, Archie has been shown to be suprisingly durable. During the Scraptrap Arc, he was shot three times, and wasn't harmed that badly.

Devil Fruit

Archie ate the Nagi Nagi no Mi during the Coliseum Island Arc and gained the ability to calm vibrations around himself. He primarily uses his ability to silence the area around himself to quiet his gun, and improve his stealth.

Archie also has another technique Sound Tunnel which he uses when he wants to have a private conversation with another person by sculpting his calmed area to only include his and the person he wishes to speak to's head

Marksmanship

Archie is a skilled marksman, being able to drop lower ranked marines with a single shot.

Explosive

Archie has an affinity for blowing things up as distractions, as demonstrated with the Bakery and the Weapons Factory.

History

Past

Archie was born in the Briss Kingdom, where he lived until he was nineteen. While living in the kingdom, he studied at the Naval Academy until he met his first captain, Maxwell, and joined him in being a pirate.

When Archie was twenty-one, the crew made their first attempt to enter the grand line, where they were beaten down by a marine captain, however they managed to escape with no casualties.

When Archie was twenty-four, his crew attempted to raid a Marine Base, hoping to take revenge on the Marine Captain. However, they failed and the Captain who origionally defeated them had been promotted to Commodore and he destroyed Archies old crew, as well as captured Maxwell and the Navigator.
