Reverse Mountain Arc

The Reverse Mountain Arc is the fifth story arc in the West Blue Saga of One Piece DnD, and it details the crews hunt for a Log Pose. The Arc is dividend into 2 sections, the first is the battle against Jack, the second is the crossing of Reverse Mountain.
