Perimus Arc

The Perimus Arc is the Second story arc in the Paradise Saga of One Piece DnD, and it explores the New Game Pirates action in the sunken Castle of Perimus. The Arc is split into three sections, the first is the crew traveling through the Perimus storm. The second is their exploration of the castle and rescue of the stranged crew, and the third is the crews journey into the Beacon room.
