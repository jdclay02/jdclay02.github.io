Parkland Arc

The Parkland Arc is the first story arc in the Paradise Saga of One Piece DnD, and shows the New Game Pirates explore the Festival Island of Parkland. The Arc is split into four sections, the first story meeting with [Kermit] while on the sea. The second is the arrival at the Pirates Paradise, the Third is the first day of events, and the fourth is the assault on the Bounty Hunter Storehouse.
