Coliseum Games Arc

The Coliseum Games Arc is the second story arc in the South Blue Saga of One Piece DnD, and it details the first island the crew visits after getting their ship. The Arc is split into three sections, the first story is the crew fighting in Low Class. The second is their fight to become middle class. The third is the crew entering High Class and escaping from the island after the Attack of Long Jack.
