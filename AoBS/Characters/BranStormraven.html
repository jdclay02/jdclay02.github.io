Brandon Stormraven, also known as Bran, is an Illuskan Human Wizard .

Bran's family hails from Neverwinter, where they own and operate a Tavern. He set out from Neverwinter after being hired on by a group of adventurers to assist in guarding a shipment of goods to Phandalin. He left with the secondary goal of finding his two missing sisters.
